Meet Walker — Software Engineering Intern

June 6, 2019

Tell us about yourself

Hi, my name is Walker Sutton. I’m currently a student at Northeastern University studying computer science in Boston, MA. I grew up in a small town on Long Island. My mother is a chef, so I enjoyed learning how to cook whilst growing up. I think British English is funny. I like to ride my bike and I like to challenge myself.

Some hobbies of mine:

  • Kiteboarding: I discovered the sport by watching videos of people kiteboarding online. I like the simplicity of solely using the wind as your power source. A few years ago, I decided to take lessons. I soon after bought some gear, but since then, I haven’t had a lot of time to go out and ride, but I’m eager to.
  • Crossword puzzles: I religiously attempt the NYT puzzles, but have only successfully been able to complete up to the Wednesday edition.
  • Making videos: Since I was a kid, I’ve always enjoyed picking up a camera and making little movies. I upload my creations to my YouTube channel.
